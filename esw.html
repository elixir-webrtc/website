JOIN US FOR 5 DAYS OF EXPERT-LED ELIXIR BROADCASTS
October 21-25, 2024 | Remote | Time: Flex
Elixir Stream Week By Elixir Devs for Elixir Devs
Five days. Five streams. Five top Elixir experts. Everything streamed using Elixir WebRTC. Grab a drink of your choice and join us to see what they have to share.
Join us
Who's hitting the virtual stage? In this first edition of Elixir Stream Week, you'll gain insights from top Elixir developers and discover how to leverage Elixir for real-time multimedia
October 21st (Mon.) 7 PM CEST
José Valim Projects that never were (part 2)
A continuation of the latest Jose keynote from ElixirConf US. "Projects that never were" are projects that Dashbit Team discussed and built proof of concept for, but ultimately decided not to move forward with them.
October 22nd (Tues.) 7 PM CEST
Mateusz Front Boombox live coding session
Boombox is a new tool built on top of Membrane to make it easier to start your journey with multimedia. A couple of weeks ago, Mateusz announced its first release and this stream will give you some overview of the Boombox API.
October 23rd (Wed.) 7 PM CEST
Chris McCord Live-coding real-time Twitch with LiveView, Elixir WebRTC and Fly.io
Chris will show you how easy it is to build a real-time Twitch clone, make it scalable and distribute it using LiveView, Elixir WebRTC and Fly.io.
October 24th (Thurs.) 7 PM CEST
Jonatan Kłosko Q&A about Livebook, Elixir Nx and Bumblebee
Ever wanted to ask a question related to Elixir's ML libraries? This will be a perfect occasion. Jonatan can't wait to respond to all of your questions!
October 25th (Fri.) 7 PM CEST
Filipe Cabaço Real-time system using Elixir - Supabase stories
Case studies of Elixir usage in Supabase. Learn how they scale to millions of messages per second. At the end, of course, Q&A session!
Come and join us! Join us for 5 days of expert sessions on Elixir WebRTC and gain insights from leading Elixir developers. You’ll also get links to the recordings, so you can rewatch them at your convenience.
